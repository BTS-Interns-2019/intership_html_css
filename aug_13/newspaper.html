Petra with love

August 13, 2019

The pet friendly trend is everywhere and Blue Trail Software could not be the exception. Being spaces where so much stress is accumulated by the workload, several companies have opted to allow workers to take their pets to promote a better working environment. From this we have the beautiful example of Petra, since his presence in the office helps interns too much, releasing stress, improving productivity, encouraging creativity, encouraging dialogue and connection between colleagues, since Petra attracts reflectors and It is inevitable to give him all the love.
