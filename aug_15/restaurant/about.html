About AOISAKANA

AOISAKANA its a small Japanese Cuisine Restaurant, which brings popular dishes of the Land of the Raising Sun to Mexico.

Japan is a place where the seafood is part of its culture. Most of its dishes consists in seafood, rice and vegetables.

The name was based of one of the most common seafood, the fish (魚, sakana) and the color of the place that the fishes locate (the water), the blue color (青, ao). So, the join of this two elements creates a blue fish, in japanese 青い魚 "aoisakana".
