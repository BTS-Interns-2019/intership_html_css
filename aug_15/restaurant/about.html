Our whereabouts?

Well, that's a tricky question. We can only say that the Mossdrop Inn is not a place to be found. It founds you. But if we want to narrow it down, head over to the Gracerich Woods on the western side of the city of Phadarel.

Be sure to come with an empty stomach and a craving for deliciousness! The Mossdrop Inn knows when it is time to show up to a hungry wanderer. So walk a bit around the forest, and when you see a golden gleam and can smell the burning logs of oaks, know that you are here.
