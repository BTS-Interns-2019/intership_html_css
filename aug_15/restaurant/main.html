What is this place, you ask?

Located at the depths of the Gracerich Woods, the Mossdrop Inn appears in the middle of an unexpected glade covered by golden grass and the warming smell of wood in the fireplace. Its big wooden doors always open to receive fearless adventurers and hardworking commoners alike. Here, you can find food and drinks that promise to replenish your energy, fill your stomach and draw a satisfied grin on your face.

It's like finding a treasure at the end of a quest. I love coming to the Mossdrop Inn in every chance I have. I have even found other kings at the tables here!

-- King Serf Rainald, ruler of Sorenth

So don't linger by the door! Coats out and knives up while the furnace is hot. Let's have ourselves a feast!
